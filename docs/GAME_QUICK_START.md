## Troubleshooting

### Game Won't Load
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Make sure you're in a chat with a match

### Can't Submit Statements
- Fill in all 3 text boxes
- Mark one statement as the lie
- Check character limit (200 per statement)

### Waiting Too Long
- Your match might be busy
- They'll get a notification
- You can close and come back later
- The game saves your progress
